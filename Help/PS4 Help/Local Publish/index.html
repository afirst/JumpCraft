Welcome to the JumpCraft Website!

 

 
What is JumpCraft?
JumpCraft is the new leading tool to create high-quality 2D platform games. The editors are very powerful and easy to use, equipped with a user-friendly interface, and do not require any coding, although that is among one of its many features.

What kinds of features does it have?
The demo version and the commercial versions are equipped with a fully-functioning map editor, tileset editor, window editor, action editor, and more.
The commercial version features game compilation and setup wizards, so you can distribute your game in a single compressed executable.

Why should I use JumpCraft?
First and foremost, because it is fun! Just think if you were to show your friends that you created an exciting platform game!  Not only that, but even developing it is fun! Instead of working on debugging tens of thousands of lines of code, you can spend your time creating and perfecting levels and graphics, and playing them.

In addition, if you purchase JumpCraft, you can even make money off of it. It’s a win-win situation!

For those who are already thinking about creating platform games, including programmers, we highly recommend you to consider JumpCraft, because of its ease of use, game templates, and its extensive (playable!) samples. Using JumpCraft can cut down your development time by more than 10 times.

Finally, the high quality games that it is capable of generating are comparable to popular platform games that have already been released, if not better! The overall quality of JumpCraft-powered games is much better than most attempts made by programmers.
